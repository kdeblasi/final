  • THE RISE OF FOOD BLOGGING

    Nowadays people go to certain restaurants in NYC simply for the aesthetic. Instagram and other forms of social media are highly influencing people to come and eat at these specific places. There has been a rise of food bloggers and it should make us question: Are millennials going to these restaurants for the experience or for social media? Think about how many pictures of overpriced avocado toasts you come across on Instagram daily. This is one example that shows how millennials view this food as something that is visually appealing, an image for their Instagram aesthetic. A lot of these "trendy" foods we see on Instagram are sometimes even overpriced for what they are. Food bloggers have the power to tempt us with any food that is pleasing to the eye.
